Overcoming Hurdles

warning Challenge: Battery Optimization

Continuous GPS tracking was draining driver device batteries in less than 4 hours.

The Engineering Solution

We implemented geofencing-based polling rates—decreasing frequency when stationary and increasing during active trips, resulting in a 60% reduction in battery consumption.

bolt Challenge: Surge Pricing Latency

Dynamic pricing calculations were lagging, causing price mismatches between request and confirmation.

The Engineering Solution

Shifted logic to the edge via AWS Lambda@Edge, caching heat-map data locally to provide instant, immutable quotes during the 60-second booking window.
